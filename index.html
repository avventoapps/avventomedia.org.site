AVVENTO MEDIA is a non-profit organization focused on Flying the everlasting gospel of the three angels of Revelation 14:6-12 to the whole world through media (text, audio and video)

Objectives

  1. To produce, promote, preserve and publish biblical sanctity.
  2. To uphold and inculcate biblical ethical values in the society.
  3. To produce digital content for building hope and sharing experiences.
  4. To proclaim the everlasting gospel.
  5. To establish a one stock center for biblical publications.

Core values

  • Integrity
  • Professionalism
  • Transparency
  • Accountability
  • Consistency
  • Dependability
  • Loyalty
  • Creativity
  • Efficiency
